=== RAW PLAN (R_i extracted actions) ===
{raw_plan}

The code-based capability filter removes actions that are infeasible for a vision-language model:
- Image manipulation (enhance, denoise, super-resolution)
- External tools (software, APIs, dictionaries)
- Human intervention (experts, proofreaders)
- Data acquisition (new photos, scans, microscope)

Feasible actions retained:
- Re-observation of image regions
- Linguistic/contextual reasoning
- Character-level re-examination
